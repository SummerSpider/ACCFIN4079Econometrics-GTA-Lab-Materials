## Lab 3 – Multivariate Regression and Asset Pricing Models
In this lab, you will 
- study multivariate regression models with a focus on asset pricing applications.
- implement the CAPM and Fama–French three-factor model in R.
- estimate coefficients using OLS, construct confidence intervals, and perform hypothesis tests on individual and joint parameters.
- be able to interpret regression results in both statistical and economic terms.
