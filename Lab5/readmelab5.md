## Lab 5 – Time Series Modeling and Forecasting
In this lab, you will l
- earn how to model and forecast financial time series using ARIMA models.
- practice the Box–Jenkins approach, identify models using ACF and PACF, select models using information criteria, and evaluate residual diagnostics.
The lab also introduces out-of-sample forecasting and stationarity testing, equipping you with core tools for time-series analysis in finance.
